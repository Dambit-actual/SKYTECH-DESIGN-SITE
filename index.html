Services

Structured aerial capture focused on visual documentation. No structural assessment, engineering opinion or compliance reporting is provided.

Aerial Property Documentation

High resolution imagery of roofs, facades and surrounding areas. Suitable for pre-works records, progress tracking, marketing angles and insurance documentation.

  • Orbit overview video of the property
  • Nadir stills for plan style context
  • Directional oblique images of key elevations
  • Curated high resolution stills of requested features

Visual Roof Survey (Imagery Only)

Focused capture for roof visibility only. Built for roofers, builders and assessors who need to see detail clearly but handle diagnosis and reporting themselves.

  • Nadir grid over the roof area
  • Oblique passes from each side for edge visibility
  • Close range passes where safe and legal
  • Organised folders and GPS tagged imagery

Aerial Mapping and Imaging

Nadir photo grids for mapping, measurement and photogrammetry workflows. Suitable for construction sites, stockpiles and general site documentation.

  • Nadir grid flown with controlled overlap
  • Orthomosaic export on request
  • Optional elevation or 3D data where supported
  • Imagery sets ready for external processing tools

Standard deliverables on each job

A consistent set of outputs so your trade, assessor or internal team knows what to expect every time.

Orbit overview
A 360 degree orbit video for context and general orientation around the site or structure.
Nadir imagery and orthomosaic
Straight down images flown in a grid, processed into an orthomosaic on mapping jobs or kept as raw input for your own tools.
Directional obliques
Angled images captured from multiple sides to expose edges, interfaces and roof fixtures.
High resolution still set
A curated selection of clear stills of key areas, all with GPS EXIF and timestamps.
Organised folders
Orbit, nadir, orthomosaic, obliques and stills separated into clearly named folders.
Imagery only
No structural assessment, defect diagnosis or compliance statement is made. Interpretation remains with qualified professionals.

Scope and limitations

Skytech Design provides visual documentation only. No part of these services constitutes:

  • a structural inspection
  • engineering advice or certification
  • insurance assessment or opinion
  • compliance sign off under any code

This is deliberate so that your own trusted trades, engineers or assessors remain responsible for judgments based on the imagery.

Regulatory Compliance

This business operates in accordance with regulations issued by the Civil Aviation Safety Authority. Current operations are primarily conducted under the CASA Excluded RPA Category using sub-2 kg aircraft within standard operating limitations.

The operator holds a current Remote Pilot Licence (RePL) and is qualified for higher-category operations up to sub-25 kg where required. All flights are conducted within approved operational limits and are fully covered by public liability aviation insurance. Certification and insurance documentation are available upon formal request.
